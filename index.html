How to play

Catch the falling colors and score points! On top of the screen you see a secondary color (green, orange or purple) that should be mixed. You are being assigned one primary color, and your job is to catch the other primary color needed to mix the secondary color. Example: You are assigned red, the target color is purple - so you have to catch a blue, to score points! Watch out for special elements, as they can either support your winning streak or end the game! If you're doing well, you'll enter hard mode, making it even trickier to catch the right color.
